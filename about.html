Who am I?

If you didn't get it from the homepage, I'm Benedykt Staples (username: bene_dictator) and I study law at the University of Canterbury in Christchurch, NZ. I like to write and edit stories, and was for a long time a content reviewer on the open source game Endless Sky. I am also a 3d modeller using Blender, soon hoping to learn Fusion360 to be able to design parts for my other hobby: nerf blaster modding and electrical soldering.

Self portrait after a couple chapters of Drawing on the Right Side of the Brain
